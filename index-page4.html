Answers to questions

  • What is the purpose of confirmations in the Bitcoin system?

    It depends on the workload of the service. When receiving Bitcoin coins, the transfer must be included in a block for confirmation. One inclusion in a block is one confirmation. When there are two or more confirmations, the transfer is considered confirmed, and Sowa operators process the request

  • How much time is given to create an application?

    You are given 15 minutes to create an application. If for some reason you do not manage to create the application within the specified time, the application will automatically disappear. In this case, please create a new application

  • How long does it take to process an application?

    It takes from 10 to 60 minutes to process one application. It depends on the workload of the service

  • What is a destination tag (memo)?

    Memo and Destination Tag are additional identifiers for your wallet/account. As a rule, exchanges provide users with one common wallet for replenishment, and then distribute the funds received in accordance with the specified Memo. If you do not specify it, your funds will go to the exchange wallet, but it will not be able to redirect them directly to your account. Make sure you enter the correct Payment ID, Memo, or Destination Tag in destinations such as EOS, Ripple (XRP), Stellar Lumens (XLM), Binance Coin (BNB) or Monero (XMR)
